Hello, I'm Zach! I'm a junior student at Portland State University pursuing a Computer Science degree with a keen interest in DevOps and Backend Engineering. I am fascinated by the process of automating software development, deployment, and infrastructure management.

In addition to DevOps, I am deeply passionate about Backend Engineering. I enjoy working on the server-side of applications, designing efficient and scalable systems, and implementing robust APIs. Building the foundation and ensuring smooth data flow in an application is what drives my enthusiasm.

Apart from my keen interest in technology, I am also deeply immersed in music. I enjoy playing many instruments; at present, my focus and obsession revolve around two areas: modular synthesis and programming rhythm machines. Over the years, I have dedicated myself to writing, recording, and performing original music as a solo artist and with various bands.

Currently, I live in the beautiful city of Portland with my wife and our dog Frances (as seen in the picture). Originally from the Midwest, I have found my home and deep appreciation for life in the Pacific Northwest. I live for morning coffee, spicy foods, good sci-fi, and spending quality time with friends and family.
